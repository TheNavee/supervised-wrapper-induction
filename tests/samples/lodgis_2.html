    Neighborhood

    Neighborhood's ambiance : prestigious

    Station : Charles de Gaulle-Etoile

    Located within Paris 17, the Ternes - Péreire district extends as far as Porte Maillot, La Place de l'Etoile and the Boulevard Gouvion Saint-Cyr. Once part of the parish of Clichy, it has gradually become one of the most fashionable and exclusive areas of Paris. Characterised by its wide avenues, charming walkways, beautiful mansions, squares and ever-popular bars, Ternes - Péreire is a quiet area with a host of local shops. The Bois de Boulogne is just a stone's throw, and other areas of the city are all within easy reach via the métro.
