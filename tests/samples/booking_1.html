One of our best sellers in Paris! Located in Paris, within a 12-minute walk of Paris Expo – Porte de Versailles and 2.4 miles of Eiffel Tower, Edgar Suites Expo Paris Porte de Versailles offers accommodations with free WiFi.

Accommodations are fitted with air conditioning and features a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. A microwave, a fridge and stovetop are also offered, as well as an electric tea pot and a coffee machine.

Parc des Princes is 2.4 miles from the apartment, while Rodin Museum is 2.8 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Orly, 10 miles from Edgar Suites Expo Paris Porte de Versailles, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.

Opened in March 2018, Edgar Suites Thoreton is 7 minutes from Parc des Expositions, Paris Versailles and Georges Pompidou Hospital.  Edgar Suites Thoreton offers different categories of suites and apartments designed and arranged to offer tenants absolute comfort. The services offered are the perfect combination between a boutique-hotel with services and a comfortable Parisian pied-à-terre, decorated with care. All our units are equipped with excellent bedding, plenty of storage space, quality linens and towels, toiletries and full kitchen and appliances equipment (Nespresso machine, microwave, dishwasher, kettle, toaster…). The apartments have an optimal wifi network specific to the apartment, a smart TV and a powerful heating and air conditioning system. They also have USB ports and universal adapters. The metro Lourmel (line 8) is only 3 minutes walk and serves in particular the Eiffel Tower (Champs de Mars), the Invalides, Concorde (Louvre and Champs Elysees), Opera Garnier, Bastille.

Edgar Suites offers custom suites and apartments accessible to a large clientele. Our spaces are designed to accommodate business clients, couples, holidaymakers, groups and large families. The services offered are the perfect combination of a boutique-hotel with services and a comfortable, well-decorated Parisian pied-à-terre. Edgar Suites places importance on the well-being of its tenants and strives to improve the daily stays of its guests. New, modern experience of connected home stays. Check-ins are by appointment, suites have no reception as a hotel neither key nor card but locks code allowing access 24/24. We offer a range of services different from traditional hotels with a more personalized, more user-friendly approach. Our multilingual team will respond to all your requests in record time 7/7.
